Testing your icon/cursor/animation

To test your icon or cursor, try the following:

  • Select the page which you want to test how it would look over various backgrounds. (If you are testing an animation, this does not apply.)

  • Choose Icon | Test..., use the magnifying glass button on the document toolbar, or press Shift+Ctrl+T.

  • Move the cursor over the appearing image to test your work.

  • You can also click and drag to draw a freehand line, to test your document as a static or animated cursor. This line can be cleared by clicking the button Clear at the bottom-left corner. Use the other two buttons to load a custom background or revert it to the default one.

  • Close the dialog box when you have finished testing.
